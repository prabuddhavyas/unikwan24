UniKwan’s visual identity is anchored in a dark-mode editorial aesthetic that combines deep nocturnal foundations with crisp typographic clarity.

The core palette is built around midnight black and graphite surfaces (`#0a0d14`, `#0f172a`, `#1e293b`), paired with white and near-white text for strong legibility across long-form content and interface elements. Strategic accent colors are used sparingly: metallic gold (`#d4af37`) elevates premium messaging, while vivid electric blue (`#3b82f6`) drives calls to action and interactive highlights.

The system is designed to feel premium, modern, and confident. It supports layered gradients, generous spacing, and refined editorial typography across both the PHP-generated site and React component library. Plus Jakarta Sans is the principal brand typeface, delivering a clean, contemporary voice, while Onest and Satoshi provide supporting headings and expressive interface text.

This design approach emphasizes:

- dark-mode contrast for readability and visual drama
- editorial layout with strong typographic hierarchy
- premium accenting through gold and blue highlights
- consistent visual tone across `assets/css/unikwan.css`, token-driven `build/css/variables.css`, and React components in `components/react/`
